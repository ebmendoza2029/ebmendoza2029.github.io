Pangulasian Island Resort

El Nido Resorts' Pangulasian Island is an eco-luxury resort on a pristine beach, known as the "Island of the Sun," offering stunning sunrise and sunset views. With a 750-meter white sand beach and a marine sanctuary, the resort features 42 contemporary Filipino-style villas, each with a balcony, accommodating up to 3 adults or 2 adults and 2 children. Guests enjoy luxurious amenities and personalized service, with facilities including WiFi, satellite TV, a mini-bar, and coffee/tea provisions.

Miniloc Island Resort

El Nido Resorts’ Eco-Discovery resort, Miniloc, embodies a Filipino coastal village atmosphere, allowing guests to swim with Jack fish and explore diverse marine life at its house reef. It provides access to Bacuit Bay attractions, including Big and Small Lagoons and Snake Island. The resort features 47 thatch-roofed rooms, furnished with indigenous materials, wooden floors, and bamboo-woven walls, each equipped with a private veranda.

The Farm at San Benito

Located in the mountains of Lipa, Batangas, The Farm at San Benito serves as a sanctuary for health and transformation, blending nature, wellness expertise, and Filipino care. It provides an environment for individuals to reset their health, connect with others, and experience personal growth.

Astoria Palawan

Astoria Palawan is an eight-hectare resort located in Puerto Princesa, Philippines, renowned for its natural beauty and tranquility. Nestled by the Sulu Sea, the resort boasts lush forests, grasslands, and mountain views, offering guests a perfect escape from city life while enjoying nature, adventure, and culinary delights.

Club Paradise Palawan

Discovery Coron, formerly Club Paradise Palawan, is an exclusive island resort in Coron, Palawan dotted along UNESCO Biosphere Reserve. It is a tropical paradise strategically located amid Coron’s tourist attractions and exhilarating dive sites. Pristine white sand beaches, rustic and charming accommodations, colorful flora and fauna, and a myriad of outdoor activities and lounging options assure the most indulgent stay.
